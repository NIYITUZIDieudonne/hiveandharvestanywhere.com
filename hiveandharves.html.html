About Hive & Harvest

Connecting beekeepers with honey lovers since 2025

Our Mission

Our platform is designed to help beekeepers sell high-quality bee products such as honey, beeswax, and propolis directly to customers.

Our Vision

We aim to empower local farmers by giving them access to a wider market while providing customers with authentic, natural products.

Fair Trade

Inspired by the challenges faced by beekeepers, we created this platform to eliminate middlemen and ensure fair pricing.

Join Us

Join us in supporting sustainable agriculture and improving livelihoods.

Together, we can make a difference - one jar of honey at a time.
